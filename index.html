Our Services

Whether you need a one time services or are looking

Ticketing

Ticketing services allow passengers to purchase tickets from the counter and inside bus. It supports digital payment such as bKash, Nagad and other online payment systems.
Journey Planner

Journey planner shows your bus on the map. It provides the estimated arrival time of the bus through the app, eliminating the need to wait at a bus stop.
Rental

Jatri Rental is used to book vehicles for vacations and trips. Users can complete their ride booking and pricing negotiations through the app.
Fleet Management

Fleet management is for bus owners. It is used to keep track of buses, expenses and accounts all in one app.
Corporate Transport

Corporate transport is a smart solution for the daily commute of corporate employees. It includes bus tracking, dashboard and other exciting features.
